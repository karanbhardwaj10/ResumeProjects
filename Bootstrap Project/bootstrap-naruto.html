So What Exactly is NARUTO ?

NARUTO is a Popular japanese Anime(Anime as in abbreviation for Animation) based upon an Orphan Hidden-leaf-village boy who all his childhood is treated like a freak by almost everyone due to his past which he knows nothing about ! Facing all of this treatment all day 24/7 he makes up his mind to become the Hokage(The village Head) so that people will acknowledge him ! Little did he know his destiny has something waayyy more for him then become just hokage of his village !
I thought of putting links to all the things that a newcomer wont understand about Naruto like this ! but that would've just filled this with nothing but hyperlinks after every 3rd letter !
So if you're looking for an anime with a long but gripping story i'd say Naruto is the show for you ! I saw naruto when i was in 7th standard but after the staggering rise of DBZ popularity all the channels only showed DBZ or someother cartoons/anime shows In my early college days by friend recommended to start watching the show again from the scratch and after that i binged watched the whole Naruto(Naruto+Shippuden) in one month !!!!
Yes yes yes yes there are a lot of filler's which doesnt add much to the main story ! So yes if you're also thinking of watching the show and skipping the fillers ! Then you surely can it wont make any difference to your experience as a whole ! You can watch Naruto on (9Anime or gogoanime or torrent) it's free there but if you're looking for an ad free thing and willing to spend money then Netflix will do the work.
